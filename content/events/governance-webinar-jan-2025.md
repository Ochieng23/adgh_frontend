This webinar will bring together five leading experts on African constitutional law and governance to examine the resurgence of coups d'etat and unconstitutional changes of government in Africa since 2020.

The panel will discuss the ACDEG mechanisms for responding to UCGs, the role of ECOWAS and other RECs, the limits of continental sanctions, and the governance conditions that make democratic backsliding more or less likely.
