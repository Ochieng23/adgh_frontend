The inaugural Pan-African Governance Summit will bring together over 400 delegates from across Africa and the global governance community for three days of high-level dialogue, knowledge exchange, and action-oriented programming.

## Programme Overview

The Summit will feature keynote addresses from African governance leaders, thematic working groups on each of the five priority areas, a civil society forum, a youth governance lab, and a closing plenary adopting the Addis Ababa Declaration on Democratic Governance.

## How to Participate

Registration is open to governance practitioners, civil society representatives, academics, journalists, and interested citizens. For registration and programme information, contact events@adgh.org.
