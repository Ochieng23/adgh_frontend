A focused technical workshop bringing together officials from Electoral Management Bodies across EAC member states alongside civil society electoral observers to examine best practice in electoral administration, voter registration, and post-election dispute resolution.

The workshop will use ADGH's Electoral Integrity Assessment Framework as its methodological anchor and will produce an East Africa Regional Electoral Governance Action Plan.
