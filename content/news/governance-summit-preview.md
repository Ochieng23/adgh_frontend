**ADDIS ABABA, 1 December 2024** — The African Democracy and Governance Hub (ADGH) today announced that it will host the inaugural Pan-African Governance Summit from 15-17 March 2025 in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

The three-day convening will bring together over 400 democratic governance practitioners, policymakers, civil society leaders, academics, and youth representatives from all regions of the continent to chart a course for strengthened democratic governance aligned with the aspirations of AU Agenda 2063.

## Summit Themes

The 2025 Summit will focus on five thematic areas:

1. **Accelerating ACDEG implementation** — From ratification to reality
2. **Electoral governance reform** — Building institutions that deliver credible elections
3. **Anti-corruption and accountability** — Closing the implementation gap
4. **Youth and women in democratic governance** — From margin to mainstream
5. **Technology and democracy** — Civic tech for African governance
