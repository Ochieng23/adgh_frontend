The African Democracy and Governance Hub (ADGH) has announced plans for its formal continental launch in 2027, marking a significant step forward in Africa's democratic governance landscape.

The launch is expected to bring together representatives from the African Union Commission, regional economic communities, civil society partners, and governance practitioners from across the continent.

ADGH's mandate will focus on five priority areas: constitutional governance, electoral integrity, civic participation, anti-corruption, and regional governance frameworks.
