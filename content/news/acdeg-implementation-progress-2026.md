ADGH is planning a comprehensive review of the African Charter on Democracy, Elections and Governance (ACDEG) implementation across member states, scheduled for publication in 2027.

The review will examine advances in electoral management body independence, civic space trends, and constitutional governance adherence, forming the basis of a continental dialogue planned for later in the year.

The full report will be made available to network members and the public upon completion.
