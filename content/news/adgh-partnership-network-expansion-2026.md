The African Democracy and Governance Hub has announced plans to expand its continental partnership network with four new strategic partners in 2027.

The Kenya Women Parliamentary Association (KEWOPA), Federation of Women Lawyers in Kenya (FIDA-Kenya), Vocal Africa, and the Center for Multiparty Democracy are expected to join the network, bringing expertise in gender and governance, legal advocacy, civic media, and multiparty political development.

These partnerships will strengthen ADGH's capacity to deliver inclusive, evidence-based governance programming across the continent once operations are fully underway.

For partnership enquiries, contact partnerships@adgh.org.
